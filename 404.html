Symptoms Of COVID-19

Common symptoms, which most often begin 2 to 14 days after you come into contact with the virus, include.

shortness of breath

Experiencing breathing difficulty describes discomfort when breathing and feeling as if you can’t draw a complete breath.

Cough

Coughing is your body’s way of getting rid of an irritant. When something irritates your throat or airway.

Fever

Fever It describes a body temperature that’s higher than normal.

Headache

Headaches can be more complicated than most people realize. Different kinds can have their own set of symptoms.

Chills

A walk down a cold, windy street can send a shivery tingle up and down your body, but so can some health conditions.

Fatigue

It may seem obvious but you could be getting too little sleep. That can negatively affect your concentration and health.
